About Me

Hi, My name is Ali, and I recently completed my Computer Science degree from the University of Hull and achieved a First class BSc with Honours. During my degree, I spread out within the different disciplines within Computer Science - from electronics and AI to Web Development and working with Agile in a team.

I spend most of my free time learning new things, including PCB design, 3D modelling and Video Game Development. I am currently working on a custom macro-pad keyboard, learning 3D modelling tools such as Blender for my game development and Fusion 360 for my more technical designs.

From a development standpoint, I am in the early design stages for a web app. The app will function as a more complex bookmarking system. The idea for this app started during my time researching and writing my dissertation. I found it very frustrating that I had to keep many tabs open in my browser, usually for an extended amount of time. Thus I came up with the idea of having a web app that keeps track of browsing sessions and keeps a description of all of the websites they are currently browsing.
